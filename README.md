# Moment by Moment

*Drawing closer to God, one moment at a time.*

A free, personal Bible companion — a quiet space to meet God in a passage, understand it, reflect, pray, and journal. Built to meet you exactly where you are.

## What it does

- **Three ways in** — tell it what you're carrying and let a passage find you, open to a passage at random, or bring a reference from your own physical Bible.
- **A guided rhythm** — every passage moves through *pray → read → understand → reflect → apply → respond*, so you're never staring at a blank page.
- **Journal, verses, and prayers** — bank what moves you, and later mark prayers answered and look back on how God has been faithful.
- **A Scripture map** — a gentle, no-guilt picture of how much of the Bible you've spent time in.
- **A reading plan** — move through the whole Bible at your own pace, in the order that suits you (straight through, chronological, or a daily mix). No calendar, no falling behind — it simply waits for you.
- **My Profile** — your spiritual portrait in one place: your Sacred Pathways temperament, spiritual gifts, and personality, plus the option to add your own photo. Retake it anytime, because seasons change.
- **Choose your translation** — the World English Bible is built in and free. Add your own free [API.Bible](https://scripture.api.bible) key in Settings to unlock NLT, NKJV, and ESV.
- **A living companion, optionally** — add your own Anthropic API key in Settings for deeper, personalized reflection questions and passage context. The app works fully without one.

## About your data — please read this part

**Your journal lives only in your browser, on this device.** There's no account and no server. Nothing you write is sent anywhere — only the Bible passage is fetched, and (if you choose to add a key) the AI reflection itself.

That also means: **if you use the app on more than one device, or you clear your browser's data, your journal won't follow automatically.** Your photo and profile are stored the same way.

To carry it with you, or to keep a backup:

1. Open the app → **Settings** → **Export backup**. This downloads a small `.json` file with everything — reflections, verses, prayers, your Scripture map, your reading plan, and your profile.
2. Save that file somewhere safe (a synced folder, or email it to yourself — whatever's easiest).
3. On another device, open the app → **Settings** → **Restore backup** → choose that file.

Make this a light habit — export after a meaningful session, or once a week — rather than something you only think about after it's too late.

## Privacy

There is no tracking, no analytics, and no account. Your writing, your prayers, and your photo never leave your device unless *you* export them. Any API keys you add are stored only in your browser and used only to talk directly to those services.
